About the Facilitator

▼

Patricia Liu is the facilitator of the NTU EMBA Generative AI Learning Group and a member of the NTU EMBA Mountaineering Club. She holds an EMBA degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and has extensive experience both in front of and behind the camera in television production.

She served as an AI advisor for the Global Warming series on Follow Me Go! (下課花路米), an award-winning children's program on Taiwan’s Public Television Service (公共電視台), contributing to bilingual content development, project planning, and visual restoration.

Passionate about exploring new ideas, Patricia combines proactive learning with a keen eye for aesthetics.
